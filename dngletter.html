I know that writing a letter and including it in the package would be much easier...But this is more fun, more aesthetically pleasing (hopefully), and gives me freedom to do more things (like include links to cat videos.) Anyways, I figured since I am no longer around the office to provide candy, I would send you some from Sweden!

I included a bunch of different candies for variety, but Kex is my favorite. I've been eating it as long as I can remember. My other favorite is Diam. It's very comparable to a Heath bar - but better. The most popular chocolate in Sweden is Marabou and it's pretty darn good too. Basically, Swedish candy is better than American candy. I tried as hard as I could to find Swedish Fish but belive it or not, they pretty much don't exist here. My mom said she'd never had Swedish Fish until she moved to the USA (When she was about 20.)

I hope you enjoy - do your best to not eat it all in a day. Keep NOCing out all the tickets (see what I did there?) and doin' stuff the DNG way.


Hope to see you all soon,
